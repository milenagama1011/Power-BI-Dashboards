## Dashboard 1: SMEs Digital Marketing Performance KPIs

Link:  
https://app.powerbi.com/reportEmbed?reportId=d20f06b9-b494-4f64-87c8-9197600e39ec

Description:  
Interactive dashboard analyzing digital marketing performance for SMEs. Tracks engagement, ad spend efficiency, audience reach, and campaign effectiveness across industries and marketing channels to support budget optimization and ROI improvement.

Data Source(s):  
Simulated marketing datasets (Excel/CSV), transformed using Power Query and Power BI data modeling.

Key Metrics:
- Total Engagement
- Total Ad Spend
- Audience Reach
- Cost per Engagement (CPE)
- Campaign Success Rate

Key Features:
- Industry and country filters
- Drill-down by company size
- Channel comparison
- Budget sliders
- Interactive time-series trend analysis

Business Impact:  
Helps marketing teams identify the most cost-effective channels, optimize budget allocation, and improve campaign performance using data-driven decisions.
